About

Welcome, I’m Hillary and I’m a counsellor based in Leeds and offer online and in person appointments.

I am an integrative Counsellor which means I am trained in a range of theories and approaches which I draw on to tailor the way I work to meet your individual needs. No matter what has led you to consider counselling, I'm here to work with you to explore your thoughts and feelings.

For individuals

Our sessions are designed to help you achieve the outcomes you desire from counselling. I offer a safe, non-judgmental and confidential space where we can work at a pace comfortable for you. This may involve delving into current and past issues and unravelling the complexities of your present relationships and experiences.

If you're looking for understanding, support, and a pathway forward, I'm here to support you on your journey.

I have experience working with anxiety and low mood/depression, My speciality is being able to sit with clients in the emotional pain they are experiencing and be alongside them whilst making sense of it together. I draw upon a range of techniques and methods to do this and always work at the pace the client feels most comfortable with. I have a strong drive to help clients work towards self- understanding, self-compassion and empowerment.

For organisations

I have worked in the third sector or Voluntary and Communities sector for 20 years. During this time I have developed community projects, volunteered, managed and delivered city-wide projects and been a trustee.

I am very aware of rising costs and short-term contracts as well as increased complexity of work have on mental health and motivation. I am keen to work with third sector organisations by offering tailored support for their staff during these incredibly challenging times.

As a qualified Counsellor and Coach, my approach involves working on a one-to- one basis with individuals who require support. I focus on the individual and their experience, taking into account the unique interplay between work and home life. My approach is friendly, supportive and collaborative. By integrating Counselling and Coaching I work in both a person-centred and solution-focused way to help the client work through issues such as:

Bereavement Stress Depression Work-life balance Anxiety Confidence Returning to work Compassion fatigue

Please contact me if you’d like to find out more about how I could work with your organisation.
